We use your personal information to operate, provide, develop, and improve the products and services that we offer our customers. These purposes include:

When you use our voice, image, and camera services, we use your voice input, images, videos, and other personal information to respond to your requests, provide the requested service to you, and improve our services. For more information about our voice services, click here.
